# 🚘 Insurance Claims Analysis & Interactive Dashboard  
### 📊 Data-driven insights for optimizing claims and risk management

---

## 📌 Overview  
This project focuses on analyzing car insurance claims data to identify patterns, trends, and risk factors.  

The analysis is presented through **interactive dashboards and visual storytelling**, enabling stakeholders to make informed decisions on claims processing, pricing, and customer strategies.

---

## 🎯 Objective  
- Explore and analyze insurance claims data  
- Identify factors driving higher claim rates  
- Build interactive dashboards for visualization  
- Provide actionable insights for business improvement  

---

## 📂 Data Description  

The dataset includes:

- **Accident & Claims Data** – Claim frequency and claim amounts  
- **Policy Details** – Policy type and coverage  
- **Customer Information** – Segmentation and profiles  
- **Risk Factors** – Variables influencing accident likelihood  

---

## 🛠️ Approach  

- Data Cleaning & Preparation  
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)  
- Data Visualization  
- Dashboard Development (Storyboarding)  
- Insight Generation & Business Recommendations  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights  

- Claims Frequency Analysis  
- Claim Amount Distribution  
- Risk Factor Analysis  
- Customer Segmentation  
- Policy Performance Insights  

---

✔ Developed interactive dashboards for claims analysis
